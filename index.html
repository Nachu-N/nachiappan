About Me

Hello! I'm Nachiappan Nachiappan, pursuing a Master of Computing in Infocomm Security at the National University of Singapore, with a focus on applying machine learning to cybersecurity challenges.

This is demonstrated through projects and research, such as developing ML models for deepfake detection and authoring a paper on ML-based anomaly detection in cybersecurity. My primary goal is to build secure systems and leverage AI to solve complex security problems.

This technical foundation is strengthened by hands-on skills with SIEM platforms like Splunk and consistent participation in competitive CTF hackathons. I focus mainly on Machine Learning applications in cybersecurity, Web development, IoT systems, and Data Analytics.
